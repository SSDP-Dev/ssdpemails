MAKING SENSE

Midwest Region

  • Our chapter leader at the University of Cincinnati is the field director for OSA's ballot campaign to reduce drug offenses from felonies to misdemeanors and has organized multiple signature drive events across campuses with his team.

Mountain Region

  • University of Colorado Boulder met with their school's residence hall director last year to ask that more harm reduction oriented materials be taught to new students, and were just informed that the association had begun using harm reduction as a philosophy for all trainings this academic year.

Southeast Region

  • Rollins College Chapter Leader Zechariah Hilles was interviewed by the campus newspaper on his chapter's efforts to remove negative stigmas around psychedelic substance use and psychedelic therapy.

Mid-Atlantic Region

  • George Mason University co-hosted the Virginia Cannabis Forum on their campus with Virginia NORML
  • An officer from the Howard chapter has been participating in a student led occupation of an administrative building on campus which has lasted over a week. The action is in protest to perceived misappropriation of funds by school administration.
