Philippines

United States

  • University of Texas Austin SSDP hosted a Naloxone Kit Making Party. Thirty-five volunteers assembled 600 naloxone kits with three doses of naloxone in each.
  • University of Texas Austin SSDP hosted a "Dungeons and Drag Queens" themed benefit party with five hundred attendees, raising $1,900.
